PROJECT 01

Shiksha

school EDUCATION SUPPORT

From 2 Students to 50+ Children

Amaanitvam Foundation started Project Shiksha with just 2 students. Through continuous efforts, community support, and dedicated volunteers, the project has now impacted more than 50 children.

Many children were unable to secure admission in government schools due to a lack of proper documentation and guidance. The foundation supported these children and their families by:

  • Assisting with school admission procedures
  • Preparing and arranging required documents
  • Guiding parents through the admission process
  • Ensuring children gained access to quality education

Today, these children are continuing their education and moving toward a brighter future.

PROJECT 02

Manthan

psychology COUNSELLING & AWARENESS

Guidance, Counselling & Positive Change

Under Project Manthan, Amaanitvam Foundation worked with children and adolescents to address negative habits and personal challenges through awareness sessions and counselling.

Initiatives included:

  • Counselling and mentorship sessions
  • Guidance on positive life choices
  • Awareness regarding harmful habits and their consequences
  • Personality development activities
  • Confidence-building sessions
  • Encouraging education, discipline, and social responsibility

Through these efforts, many children demonstrated positive behavioural changes and developed a stronger focus on their education and future goals.
