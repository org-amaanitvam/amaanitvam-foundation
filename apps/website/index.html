Vision & Mission

The principles that guide every program, partnership, and community we serve.

Vision Statement

Amaanitvam Foundation envisions a brighter and more inclusive India where every individual has access to opportunities for learning, growth, and self-development. Through education, mentorship, and community-driven action, we aim to nurture confident, responsible, and compassionate citizens who contribute positively to society and inspire lasting change.

Mission Statement

At Amaanitvam Foundation, we strive to inspire compassion, responsibility, and active citizenship. Through education, mentorship, awareness, and community engagement, we empower individuals to create meaningful change and contribute to a more inclusive, equitable, and compassionate society where everyone has the opportunity to learn, grow, and thrive.

Field Notes

Where the Work Actually Happens

Two projects, two different problems to solve — the same volunteers showing up for both.

Project Shiksha
school Education Access

The Classroom That Started With Two Chairs

Project Shiksha didn't begin as a program — it began with two children who couldn't get into a government school because their families lacked the right paperwork. Volunteers stepped in to sort out admission forms, chase documentation, and sit with parents through a process that was never built with them in mind. Word spread. What started with two students now reaches more than fifty.

"No child should be kept out of a classroom for the want of a form."

Project Manthan
psychology Counselling & Awareness

Conversations That Change Direction

Not every problem a child carries shows up on a report card. Project Manthan gives children and adolescents a space to talk — about pressure, bad habits, and the choices in front of them — through mentorship, awareness sessions, and honest conversation. It's slower, quieter work than a classroom, but the shift in confidence and focus is the whole point.

"Sometimes the most useful thing you can do is listen first."
